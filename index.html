    Mission Statement

    We work with Southeast Residents, particularly on low and fixed income, to help them access resources, support, and develop instruments to combat poverty and urban decline. We foster individual and neighborhood pride, participation and self-determination.

    Description

    A non-profit community organization providing advocacy and education, with a special dedication to the poor. We offer programs for after school, youth enrichment, summer camps for ages 6-12, leadership workshops and public health projects for teens.
